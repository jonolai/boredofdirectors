Purpose

Systemic racism in the United States can only change once the most powerful corporations diversify their Board of Directors and Executive Leadership to reflect the population of the country. This website showcases the current state of the Board of Directors of the Fortune 100 companies.

Compared to 60.4% of the population

Compared to 18.3% of the population

12.1% of Directors are Black

Compared to 13.4% of the population

3.2% of Directors are Asian

Compared to 5.9% of the population

Compared to 49.3% of the population

Compared to 50.7% of the population
